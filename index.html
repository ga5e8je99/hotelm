Top cities

sharmelshikh

Discover Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt's sun-kissed paradise! Ideal for a week-long escape, the best time to visit is between October and April for comfortable temperatures. Dive into crystal-clear waters, explore vibrant coral reefs, relax on pristine beaches, and indulge in thrilling water sports. Immerse yourself in the magic of Sharm El-Sheikh!

Top Cities

Cairo

Discover Cairo, Egypt's vibrant capital! Immerse yourself in ancient wonders like the Pyramids of Giza and the Egyptian Museum. Explore the bustling Khan el-Khalili bazaar, wander through Islamic Cairo's historic streets, and witness the serene beauty of Coptic Cairo's churches. The ideal time to visit Cairo is during the shoulder seasons: spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November). The weather is pleasant, and there are fewer crowds than during peak season. Plan to stay in Cairo for at least 3-4 days to fully experience its rich history and culture.

Top Cities

Giza

Discover Giza, a city steeped in ancient wonders and modern charm. Immerse yourself in history at the iconic Pyramids of Giza and the enigmatic Sphinx, dating back over 4,500 years. Ideal for a 3-4 day stay, visit during the cooler months (October-April) for comfortable exploration. Beyond the pyramids, Giza offers vibrant markets, delicious local cuisine, and the serene Nile River. Unravel the mysteries of the past and experience the warmth of Egyptian hospitality in this captivating city.

Top Cities

ALEXANDRIA

Discover Alexandria, Egypt's Mediterranean gem! Immerse yourself in history at the Catacombs of Kom el-Shuqqafa and Pompey's Pillar. Explore the vibrant Corniche, relax on pristine beaches, and indulge in fresh seafood. Ideal for a 3-4 day stay, visit during spring (March-May) or autumn (September-November) for pleasant weather. Unforgettable experiences await!

Top cities

Aswan

Discover Aswan, Egypt's captivating southern gem! Immerse yourself in ancient wonders like the Philae Temple and the Unfinished Obelisk. Sail the Nile on a felucca, explore vibrant Nubian villages, and witness the majestic Aswan High Dam. Ideal stay: 3-5 days. Best visit: October to April for pleasant weather. Unforgettable experiences await!

Top Cities

Luxor

Luxor, Egypt's ancient treasure trove, offers a captivating journey through time. Explore the Valley of the Kings, witness the grandeur of Karnak and Luxor Temples, and cruise the Nile. Ideal for a 3-5 day stay, visit between October and April for the best weather. Immerse yourself in history, culture, and stunning landscapes.

Top Cities

Ismailia

iscover Ismailia, Egypt's charming canal city! Ideal for a 3-4 day stay, visit between October and April for pleasant weather. Explore the Suez Canal, cruise on Lake Timsah, relax on pristine beaches, delve into history at Tel El Kabir, and savor delicious local cuisine. Ismailia offers a unique blend of history, nature, and tranquility.
